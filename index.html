STORY

Rajnikant

One of the most iconic figures in Indian cinema, Rajnikant’s rise to fame is nothing short of extraordinary. Before becoming a superstar, Rajnikant worked as a bus conductor in Bangalore. His charisma and unique style caught the eye of filmmakers, and today, he is not only a beloved actor but a cultural phenomenon. His journey reminds us that true talent, paired with perseverance, can overcome any obstacle.

Deepika Padukone

Before becoming one of Bollywood’s highest-paid actresses, Deepika was an aspiring badminton player following in her father’s footsteps. However, her passion for acting drove her to shift paths, and after a successful modeling career, she made her film debut. Today, Deepika is not only a successful actress but a global icon known for her versatility and philanthropic work.



Kangana Ranaut

Hailing from a small town in Himachal Pradesh, Kangana’s story is one of resilience and determination. She faced several challenges in her early career, including financial difficulties and rejection. Despite these setbacks, her relentless pursuit of success eventually paid off, and she is now regarded as one of the most talented and fearless actresses in Indian cinema.



Priyanka Chopra

Priyanka Chopra’s journey from a small-town girl to a global superstar is nothing short of inspiring. After winning the Miss World pageant in 2000, she transitioned into Bollywood, where she faced numerous challenges. Through hard work and determination, Priyanka not only became a leading actress in India but also successfully broke into Hollywood, becoming a global brand ambassador and producing films and TV shows through her own production house.



Nawazuddin Siddiqui

A powerhouse of talent, Nawazuddin’s journey to stardom wasn’t easy. He worked as a watchman and did several small jobs while auditioning for roles in Mumbai. After years of struggle, his raw talent finally got him recognition in films like Gangs of Wasseypur and The Lunchbox, making him one of the most critically acclaimed actors today. His story is a testament to the power of persistence and belief in oneself.

Arijit Singh

Arijit Singh, born on April 25, 1987, in West Bengal, gained fame with the song "Tum Hi Ho" from Aashiqui 2 in 2013. Known for his soulful voice, he quickly became a top playback singer in Bollywood. Despite his success, Arijit maintains a humble and low-key lifestyle. His music continues to resonate with millions worldwide.



Dwayne Douglas Johnson

Dwayne Douglas Johnson (born May 2, 1972) also known by his ring name The Rock, is an American actor, professional wrestler, and businessman. He is signed to WWE, where he performs on a part-time basis. Widely regarded as one of the greatest professional wrestlers of all time,[6][7] Johnson was integral to the development and success of the World Wrestling Federation (WWF, now WWE) during the Attitude Era.



Joaquin Phoenix

Joaquin Rafael Phoenix born October 28, 1974 is an American actor. Known for his roles as dark, unconventional and eccentric characters, particularly in period dramas, he has received various accolades, including an Academy Award, a British Academy Film Award, a Screen Actors Guild Award, a Grammy Award, and two Golden Globe Awards. In 2020, The New York Times named him one of the greatest actors of the 21st century.



Tom Cruise

Tom Cruise, Hollywood’s resident daredevil, doesn’t just earn his hefty paycheck, he literally jumps, runs, and hangs off planes for it. Who could forget the iconic building leap in ‘Mission: Impossible – Fallout’ that broke his ankle, which he finished in true Cruise style? With the release of Top Gun: Maverick in theaters worldwide in 2022, Tom Cruise has once again proved he’s the undisputable king of Hollywood.
